How We Compare

Why BW2 is the superior choice for commercial software development.

vs. Low-Code Platforms

Their Goal

To enable non-developers to build simple internal apps. You eventually hit a "low-code wall" where complex logic becomes impossible.

Our Goal

To empower professional developers. We don't replace you; we make you exponentially more productive. No walls, just code.

vs. SaaS Ecosystems

Their Goal

To lock you in. You are effectively a reseller, forced to operate within their limits. Your product is just a line item in their App Store.

Our Goal

Shared Success. You own the customer relationship. You set your prices. You build your brand. We provide the engine.

vs. Open Source ERP

Their Goal

Free code, but once you modify the core, you face the "forking problem." Updates become complex and costly merges.

Our Goal

A managed, evolving platform. Our architecture separates your custom code from our core, guaranteeing future-proof updates.

vs. Building it Yourself

The Risk

High cost, high risk, long time-to-market. You would need to invest millions to replicate infrastructure we offer for free.
