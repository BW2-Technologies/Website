About BW2

Democratizing
Software Development

Our developer platform is designed to provide unparalleled value and address the typical technical and functional challenges for small and mid-sized developer teams building business software.

We provide our complete, enterprise-grade platform to you at zero cost.

We only charge for activating the customization layer as the sole software feature.

Our Mission

Leveling the Playing Field
"

For years, our founder Cyrill Jenni built business software the classic way - and grew increasingly frustrated. He saw firsthand how the high costs, immense risks, and rigid tools of traditional development crushed innovation for small and mid-sized teams.

"BW2 was not born to create just another development tool; it was born to level the playing field."

That's why our platform is free. That's why our entire business model is built on partnership and Shared Success.

Our Vision

The BusinessWideWeb

We envision a future where the power to build comprehensive business solutions is no longer restricted to large corporations like Microsoft, Oracle, SAP or Salesforce.

We are building a global ecosystem where success isn't limited by budget, but where specialized expertise can be scaled to serve entire industries.

By removing financial and technical barriers, BW2 will become the foundational platform that powers the next generation of enterprise software.

Why Partner With BW2?

Three pillars of value for our partners.

Technical Benefits

Our platform removes the burden of modernization. It offers advanced, scalable, and secure technology with full customization possibilities and cloud-based services out of the box.

Feature Benefits

Our platform integrates standard CRM & ERP functionality, freeing you to focus on the unique features that are mission-critical to your customers and provide the highest value.

Business Model Benefits

Our Shared Success model eliminates your financial risk. The core platform is free, with no upfront licensing fees. We only succeed when you do, ensuring our goals are always perfectly aligned.
